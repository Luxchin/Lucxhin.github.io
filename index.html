Profile

My work sits at the intersection of engineering and environmental, energy and institutional challenges. I am interested in turning broad goals into technical decisions, viable projects and measurable results.

Energy transition does not end when a technology is selected. The real challenge is creating the conditions for it to be implemented, operated, maintained and able to fulfil its purpose.

The questions matter

A promising technology may still fail to deliver the expected results. These are some of the questions that guide my work:

Will it work under the real conditions of the territory?
Is the required infrastructure available?
Who will be able to operate and maintain it?
What will it cost throughout its full life cycle?
How will it integrate with existing systems?
How will we know whether it is truly fulfilling its purpose?
