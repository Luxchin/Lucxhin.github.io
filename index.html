02 / Approach

Ask the questions others skip.

A technology can look impressive and still fail. These questions keep ideas honest.

Q01Will it work under real operating conditions?
Q02Is the required infrastructure actually available?
Q03Who will operate, maintain and finance it?
Q04What happens across the full life cycle?
Q05How does it connect with the systems already in place?
Q06What evidence will prove that it works?
